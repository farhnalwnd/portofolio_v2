Hirezy is an intelligent recruitment platform designed to streamline hiring workflows for HR professionals and accelerate job discovery for candidates. 

**For HR Professionals:** The system automates bulk CV screening and ranking by evaluating uploaded resumes against job descriptions through a robust 3-component formula (similarity score, skill coverage, and domain relevance). Powered by a fine-tuned multilingual Bi-Encoder architecture ("paraphrase-multilingual-MiniLM-L12-v2"), it assesses context and true technical capability, moving far beyond simple keyword matching.

**For Job Seekers:** The platform features a vector-based semantic search that allows candidates to upload their CVs and instantly discover highly relevant job openings. Furthermore, it provides AI-driven recommendations and actionable insights to help job seekers improve their resume alignment with specific job requirements.
