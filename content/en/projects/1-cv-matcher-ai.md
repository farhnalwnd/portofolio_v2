**Hirezy** is an intelligent recruitment platform designed to streamline hiring workflows for HR professionals and accelerate job discovery for candidates.

### Key Features
- **Automated CV Screening**: Compares resumes against job requirements via a 3-component formula evaluation.
- **Multi-lingual Bi-Encoder**: Powered by a fine-tuned `paraphrase-multilingual-MiniLM-L12-v2` model for deep context parsing.
- **Vector-Based Matcher**: Semantic search for candidates to find positions matching their resume structure.
- **Actionable Recommendations**: Delivers AI-generated improvement tips for resume formatting.
